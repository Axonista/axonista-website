Product Details

The new 3Player App on Xbox Live for Xbox 360 gives Xbox Live Gold Members access to programmes from TV3 and 3e channels. The app utilises features such as gesture and voice control and is easy to navigate thanks to a sleek, uncluttered design.

The app is free for users and ad supported which means TV3's advertising partners can engage with a young, male audience through pre and mid-roll advertising around key shows.

We work with broadcasters to make TV truly interactive, connecting viewers to storytellers in engaging, measurable and monetisable ways.
