From Android to Xbox, 3player gives TV3 and 3e viewers an exclusive place for their favourite shows at home or on the move.

The Objective

The way the world consumes video has changed. Audiences watch more video on mobile than ever before. Devices with touch screens, and gesture and voice control, bring with them an opportunity to build entirely new types of viewing.

The natively developed 3player apps allow TV3 and 3e’s audience to enjoy high quality picture and intuitive design on a seamless live or on demand stream across multiple devices.

"It's great to work with innovative broadcasters like TV3 who understand the importance of creating intuitive, engaging video experiences for viewers on mobile. We were delighted to be chosen to design and develop these native player apps for our national broadcaster."

— Claire McHugh, CEO Axonista
