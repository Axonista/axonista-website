Aol Rise

Aol Rise for iPhone is the best way to start your day.

About Aol Rise

RISE aims to be your first daily digital destination. Get a heads-up on what people will be talking about today - headlines, culture, and life tips distilled and packaged into a perfect bite-sized form.

Results

  • Purchase Items On Air

    Users can purchase products anywhere in their second screen experience while keeping them in their current environment.

  • Loyalty Badging

    Rewarding enables cross platform loyalty and allows users to unlock content and earn badges for interaction.

  • Moving Content

    Enables the ability to move content from the users digital experience to their TV.

  • Customisation & Personalisation

    Ability to to recognise the customer and offer access to their wish list, favourites and QVC voices.

  • Content Based on GEO Location

    Gives the ability to provide targeted project offerings based on location while also narrowing targeted offers within 5 regions.

  • Manage Wish List

    Viewers have the ability to add and remove products to their wish list and also share products with friends.

Purchase Items On Air With Interactive Elements

Users can interact with the QVC app in order to purhcase items that are on air or that have been previously aired. Users also have the option to purchase what the host is using or wearing.

Methodology & Testing

We used C# / .Net client-side, XML, Silverlight and DOM development in this project, and we use XML and DOM in Roku channel development projects. We've developed 3player on Roku and Windows Phone as well for TV3. We provide a good design and development service that attracts repeat custom. IPTV, OTT and VOD are a key part of our product roadmap for our audience engagement CMS Ediflo, that we have developed ourselves and which supports a number of our companion viewing apps.

Product Details

In Second-screen mode the app provides the Standalone functionality plus additional content that is relevant to what they are watching right now on the primary screen, whether it’s live content or previously aired content.

Social media content and engagement opportunities will be present in all three sections of the App, but within Watch these will be highlights of social content produced during that show.
